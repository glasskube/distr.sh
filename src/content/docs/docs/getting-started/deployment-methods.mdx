Distr is a versatile platform designed to adapt to user preferences, thanks to its Open Source foundation. It offers two deployment methods: as a SaaS solution hosted, maintained by the Glasskube team or as a self-hosted option.

Letâ€™s explore the features of each deployment method:

## Distr as a SaaS

Features:

- **Maximum flexibility** with access to the latest **updates** and features.
- Simplified **upgrade** paths.
- Support for **unlimited applications**.
- **Single Sign-On (SSO)**.
- Advanced **credentials encryptio**n for enhanced security.
- **No platform management responsibilities**.

<Aside type="note" title="Info">
  Get started by creating your Distr account
  [here](https://distr.sh/) and then following the onboarding
  steps here
</Aside>

## Self-Hosted Distr

Features:

- **Complete control** over the deployed version of Distr.
- **Full responsibility for platform availability and maintenance**.
- Ideal for users who **prefer to manage their environment** independently.

<Aside type="note" title="Info">
  To get started with the self-hosted option, follow our
  [guide](/docs/self-hosting/self-hosting/)
  to deploy Distr in your environment.
</Aside>
